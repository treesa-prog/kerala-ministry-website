About

Vadassery Damodaran Satheesan is the Chief Minister of Kerala and a prominent Congress leader representing the Paravur constituency. A lawyer and social worker, he assumed the role of Chief Minister after the UDF's landslide victory in the 2026 Kerala Assembly elections, ending a decade of LDF rule. Known for his strong legislative record and advocacy for transparency and governance reforms.
