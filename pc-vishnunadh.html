About

Panamthottathil Chandrasekharan Vishnunadh is a senior Congress leader and four-time MLA representing the Kundara constituency in Kollam district. Born on 30 March 1978, he began his political journey through the Kerala Students Union (KSU) and rose through the Kerala Youth Congress. In 2026, he was appointed as Minister for Tourism and Culture — a vital appointment as Kerala surpassed 25 million tourist arrivals in 2025, the highest ever in the state's history. Known for his grassroots connect, youth-centric vision, and personal love for literature and the performing arts.
