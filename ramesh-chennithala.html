About

Ramesh Chennithala is one of Kerala's most seasoned political leaders. A senior Congress figure and former Leader of the Opposition, he has served the people of Haripad for multiple terms. Appointed Home Minister in 2026, he brings decades of administrative and legislative experience to the most powerful portfolio outside the Chief Minister's office. He previously served as Home Minister under CM Oommen Chandy (2014–2016).
