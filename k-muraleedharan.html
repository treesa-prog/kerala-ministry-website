About

Kannoth Muraleedharan is a senior Indian National Congress leader and lawyer from Kerala, known for his long and distinguished parliamentary career. Son of former Chief Minister K. Karunakaran, he has served as MP multiple times from both Kozhikode and Vatakara constituencies. In 2026 he was sworn in as Health Minister of Kerala, bringing his extensive national and state-level experience to one of the most critical portfolios in the cabinet.

Committed to strengthening Kerala's world-class healthcare system and public health infrastructure.

Parliamentary Record

Elected to Lok Sabha multiple times from Kozhikode and Vatakara — a distinguished parliamentary career spanning decades.

KPCC Leadership

Served as President of the Kerala Pradesh Congress Committee (KPCC) from 2001 to 2004, strengthening Congress organisationally.

Career Timeline

2004: Served briefly as Minister for Electricity under CM A. K. Antony
2010–2016: Member of Rajya Sabha representing Kerala
2011–2019: MLA from Vattiyoorkavu constituency
2019–2024: Elected to Lok Sabha from Vatakara
