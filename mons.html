About

Mons Joseph is a senior Kerala Congress leader and five-time MLA representing the Kaduthuruthy constituency in Kottayam district. Born on 30 May 1964 in Kaduthuruthy, he belongs to a Syro-Malabar Catholic family. His parents are O. Joseph and Mariamma Joseph, and he is married to Sonia. A lawyer by profession, Mons Joseph has served Kerala's infrastructure sector with distinction, having previously held the portfolio of Minister for Public Works on two occasions. In 2026, he was appointed Minister for Water Resources in the UDF government led by Chief Minister V. D. Satheesan, reflecting his proven track record in managing large-scale infrastructure and resource departments. He is known for his steady, experienced governance and deep commitment to the welfare of Kottayam and central Kerala.

Achievements

Water Resources Leadership

Appointed to oversee Kerala's critical water resources infrastructure, building on his earlier experience as Minister for Public Works and his deep knowledge of state infrastructure.

Infrastructure Development

Served twice as Minister for Public Works (1996–2001 and 2007–2009), leaving a lasting impact on Kerala's road, bridge, and public infrastructure landscape.

Constituency Development

Continuously re-elected from Kaduthuruthy since 1996, reflecting strong grassroots trust and consistent delivery of development to the Kottayam region.
