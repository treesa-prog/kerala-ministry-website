About

Sunny Joseph, affectionately known as "Sunnychaayan" among his supporters, is a senior Indian National Congress leader and three-time MLA representing the Peravoor constituency in Kannur district. Born on 18 August 1952 in Thodupuzha, Idukki district, he is a lawyer by profession and a graduate of Calicut University with degrees in BA and LLB. Known for his calm, soft-spoken demeanour and respected leadership style, Sunny Joseph was appointed KPCC President in May 2025, leading the Congress party in Kerala through the landmark 2026 Assembly election victory. In recognition of his pivotal organisational role and seniority, he was inducted into the Satheesan Cabinet as Minister for Electricity, Environment, and Parliamentary Affairs in May 2026.
